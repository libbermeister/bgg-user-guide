User Guide

Introduction

BoardGameGeek is the premiere hub for everything boarg game related. It has the largest community of board game enthusiasts and the most extensive database of board games. Using this site, you can...

  • Look up board games, read reviews, and find all sorts of information about games.
  • Read articles, news, and forums about board games, events, and other things.
  • Create an account to track your game collection, make trades with other users, and get involved in the community via forums and other resources.
  • Access community content to get tips, ask questions, and find cool content created by users.
  • Buy and sell games via the GeekMarket.
